Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

What we collect

Two things, and that's it:

  • Your email address — when you sign up for free access or verify your membership in the extension.
  • Basic usage events — simple signals like "verified email" or "viewed a brand," so we know what's working.

Why we collect it

Your email verifies your account (free or member) and lets us send you product updates and tips. Usage events help us improve the product. That's the whole story.

What the extension accesses

The extension reads the content of jointrybe.com pages you visit — only to recognize which brand page you're on and display brand information next to it. It does not read, track, or collect anything on any other website.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever. To anyone.
  • We do not collect your browsing history outside jointrybe.com.
  • We do not collect passwords, payment details, or personal files. Payments are handled entirely by Skool.

Email list

Emails are stored securely and synced to our email provider (Mailchimp) so we can send you updates. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link.

Deleting your data

Want out? Email us and we'll delete your email and associated data within 7 days. Contact: austingreat1991@gmail.com

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above and note it in our community.
